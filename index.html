SUMMARY

CUSTOM CRAFTED ART KITS

Who Made Who Art Kits contain physical and mental tools to boost creativity and explore the process of making. Creativity can bring about change in our daily life and in helping our communities to engage in social change.

Each Art Kit contains the tools and supplies necessary for the creator to learn and pursue a creative skill, such as block printing.

Thoughtfully designed packaging and directions include a link to a complementary app and website that will contain informational videos, creativity boosts, helpful tips, and other information.

The Art Kits can be done independently with the directions and videos. Live workshops will also be included.

The art kits encompass many of my skills and with this project I aim to further my experiences with teaching and exploring the benefits of creativity.
